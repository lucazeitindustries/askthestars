## Week 2: TikTok Launch
**Goal:** First viral content, 500 users

### Content Strategy
- 2-3 TikToks per day
- Format 1: "POV: You're a [sign] and..." + relatable zodiac meme
- Format 2: "Let AI read your birth chart" + screen recording of the app
- Format 3: "Are [sign1] and [sign2] compatible?" + app result
- Format 4: Zodiac roasts (from our roast prompt)
- Format 5: "Things your zodiac sign does" compilations

### Growth Hack: Compatibility Viral Loop
- Compatibility results page has "Share with your partner" button
- Partner needs to enter THEIR birth data to see full result
- That's a free signup for every share
- Target: 2x viral coefficient on compatibility checks

## Week 3: Paid Ads Test
**Goal:** Validate CAC, reach 1000 users

### Meta Ads ($20/day test)
- Audience: Women 18-35, interests: astrology, zodiac, horoscopes, Co-Star, birth chart
- Creative 1: "What do the stars say about your love life? 🔮" → free reading
- Creative 2: "Your AI astrologer is waiting ✨" → chat
- Creative 3: "Is your relationship written in the stars?" → compatibility

### Google Ads ($10/day test)
- Keywords: "daily horoscope", "birth chart calculator", "zodiac compatibility"
- These are cheap — most astrology sites don't bid aggressively

### TikTok Ads ($10/day test)
- Boost top-performing organic TikToks
- Spark ads format (uses organic post as ad)

## Week 4: Optimization
**Goal:** $1K MRR

### Conversion Optimization
- A/B test paywall placement (after 3 questions vs after 5)
- A/B test pricing ($7.99 vs $9.99 vs $12.99)
- Add "3-day free trial" to reduce friction
- Email capture popup on daily horoscope pages

### Retention
- Daily push notifications with personalized horoscope
- Weekly email digest
- "Mercury retrograde alert" notifications (events drive engagement)

## Month 2-3: Scale
**Goal:** $5-15K MRR

### SEO Compounding
- 170+ pages indexed and ranking
- Blog content targeting long-tail keywords
- Backlink building (guest posts on astrology blogs)

### Influencer Partnerships
- Micro-influencers in astrology niche (5K-50K followers)

## Key Metrics to Track

- SEO impressions + clicks (Google Search Console)
